Meet The Team

James 'Sugar' Stevens

Coach Stevens fosters a culture of hard work and perseverance within the gym's walls. His keen eye for technique allows him to tailor training sessions to each boxer's unique strengths and weaknesses, ensuring they reach their full potential. Under his tutelage, fighters not only learn the physical aspects of boxing but also the mental fortitude required to overcome challenges in and out of the ring. Coach Stevens embodies the spirit of Savage Strike MMA, cultivating champions who leave an indelible mark on the world of combat sports.

Mauricio Santos

Within the walls of Savage Strike MMA, Coach Santos reigns as a distinguished Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) mentor. With an unwavering passion for the gentle art, Coach Santos brings a wealth of knowledge and technical mastery to the mat. With a calm demeanor and an infectious enthusiasm, he guides his students through the intricacies of BJJ, instilling in them the importance of leverage, technique, and mental resilience.

Rachel Garcia

At Savage Strike MMA, Coach Garcia embodies the spirit of empowerment and resilience as the esteemed female freestyle coach. With a wealth of experience in the world of freestyle martial arts, she brings a unique blend of technical prowess and unwavering support to her students. Coach Garcia creates a nurturing and inclusive environment where fighters of all backgrounds and skill levels can thrive.

Duane Freeman

Coach Freeman hones their students' technique, fosters mental fortitude, and instills an unyielding work ethic. Under their guidance at Savage Strike MMA, fighters emerge as versatile warriors, armed with the devastating power of Muay Thai strikes and the technical prowess of wrestling, ready to conquer any opponent in the ring.
